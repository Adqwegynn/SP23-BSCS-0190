How it Works:
Upload - Drag & drop CV file 
Extract - The sysyten will reas text from PDF/Word
Analyze - The sysytem will compares CV with job requirements
Score - Then the sysytem will calculates match percentage
Report - Shows results and recommendations.....


ISSUES: 
1- PDF is not uploading: For that ensure that the PDF that you are uploading is in the readable text and it does not contain any kind of scanned images 

2- Analysis Takes Long Time: Large Files will take some time, for that you have to wait. 

3- No Skills Detected: Ensure your CV mentions skills clearly according to the job description 

4- Privacy notice keeps appearing:  For that Clear your browser cache and try again..
